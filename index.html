Spaghetti Bolognese

INGREDIENTS







Time

Prep: 10:00 min.

Cook: 30:00 min.

PREPARATION

Prepare the sauce: Cut the onion and the garlic piece. Fry slowly on a high pan. When the garlic take a golden color add the meet, when the meat is cooked, add the tomatoes. Leave cook for 30 minutes.
Cook the pasta: prepare a cooking pot with 3 liters of salted water. When the water boil add the spaguettis. Let cook the pasta for 8 minutes and retire.
Serve the pasta with the tomato sauce, to finish put the parmesan cheese on the sauce.
